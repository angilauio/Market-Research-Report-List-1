<p><p>The Social Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based CRM offers flexibility, easy access, and lower upfront costs by hosting data on remote servers, facilitating real-time updates and collaboration. In contrast, on-premises CRM installs software directly on a company’s servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Both types enable businesses to manage customer interactions effectively.</p></p>
