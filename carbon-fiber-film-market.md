<p><p>The carbon fiber film market encompasses three main types based on the number of layers: single-walled, double-walled, and multi-walled carbon fibers. Single-walled films are composed of a single layer, offering high tensile strength and lightweight properties, ideal for specialized applications. Double-walled films provide additional strength and structural integrity, suitable for more demanding uses. Multi-walled films, featuring multiple layers, enhance durability and thermal stability, making them ideal for advanced engineering applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
