<p><ul><li>Primer Coatings</li><li>Back Coatings</li><li>Top Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solvent-borne coil coatings market is segmented into three main types: primer coatings, back coatings, and top coatings. Primer coatings serve as an initial layer, providing adhesion and corrosion resistance. Back coatings protect the reverse side of metal substrates, enhancing durability and preventing corrosion. Top coatings, which are the final layer, offer aesthetic appeal and additional protection against UV light, chemicals, and weathering. Together, these coatings ensure the longevity and performance of metal products in various applications.</p></p>
