<p><p>Polymer Matrix Composites (PMCs) are materials made of a polymer resin reinforced with fibers, offering enhanced mechanical properties. The PMC market is primarily divided into thermosets and thermoplastics. Thermosets are rigid and strong, curing irreversibly upon heating, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. In contrast, thermoplastics remain moldable upon heating, allowing for recycling and reprocessing, which attracts applications in automotive and aerospace sectors. Both types cater to diverse industries due to their lightweight and durable characteristics.</p></p>
