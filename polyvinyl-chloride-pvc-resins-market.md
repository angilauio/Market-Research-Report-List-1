<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) resins market comprises three main types: suspension, emulsion, and blended. Suspension PVC is produced through a polymerization process, resulting in granular particles widely used in pipes and profiles. Emulsion PVC is created in aqueous conditions, facilitating a finer particle size ideal for coatings and adhesives. Blended PVC combines different resin types to enhance properties like flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for applications like flooring and automotive parts. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
