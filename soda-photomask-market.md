<p><p>The Soda Photomask Market is primarily divided into two types: Soda Chrome Masks and Soda Emulsion Masks. Soda Chrome Masks are created using a chrome layer on a glass substrate, offering high resolution and thermal stability, making them suitable for integrated circuits. In contrast, Soda Emulsion Masks utilize a photo-sensitive emulsion layer, allowing for simpler production processes and flexibility in design. Both types serve critical roles in photolithography, driving advancements in semiconductor manufacturing and other related industries.</p></p>
