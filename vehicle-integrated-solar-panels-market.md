<p><p>The Vehicle-Integrated Solar Panels market primarily features two types of silicon cells: Mono-Crystalline and Multi-Crystalline. Mono-Crystalline Silicon Cells, known for their high efficiency and sleek design, are ideal for limited space on vehicles. In contrast, Multi-Crystalline Silicon Cells are more cost-effective, offering moderate efficiency. While Mono-Crystalline cells leverage single-crystal silicon for optimal performance, Multi-Crystalline cells use multiple silicon crystals, making them suitable for budget-conscious applications. Both types enhance energy generation in vehicle-integrated solar technologies.</p></p>
