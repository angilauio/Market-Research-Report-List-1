<p><p>Induction forging heaters are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I heaters are designed for high-frequency applications, providing rapid heating for small to medium-sized components, ensuring efficiency and precision in the forging process. Type II heaters operate at lower frequencies and are suited for larger components, offering uniform heating over a broader area. Both types enhance productivity and quality in manufacturing by reducing energy consumption and minimizing thermal distortion during the forging of metals.</p></p>
